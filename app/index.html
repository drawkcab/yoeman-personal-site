Who I am

I graduated and worked as an industrial engineer before becoming a full stack web developer. I love solving problems and the gratification that comes from solving complex problems in code. Currently I am enjoying working with HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Node.js, Ruby, Rails, and Cordova. I enjoy 3D printing, traveling, and hobby electronics. My varied experiences allow me to provide a novel perspective through my problem solving and leadership skills developed as an engineer. Most importantly, I love to challenge myself with coding and continually learning.
